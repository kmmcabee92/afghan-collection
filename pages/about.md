## About the Collection

This collection consists of a selection of afghans made by Mary Carol Mussmann, my grandma. Many of these afghans were made for specific family members. After she passed away in 2017, her personal collection of full-sized and baby afghans were shared between her 3 daughters, 7 grandchildren, and (at the time) 3 great-grandchildren.
